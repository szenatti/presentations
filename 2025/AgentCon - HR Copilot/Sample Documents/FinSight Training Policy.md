# FinSight Training Policy

*Effective Date: 1 January 2025*  
*Last Updated: 31 May 2025*  
*Document ID: HR-POL-002*  

## Purpose  
To enhance FinSight’s workforce skills, supporting its mission to drive innovation through AI and financial intelligence. This policy outlines training opportunities for employees across Sales, R&D, and other departments in Australian offices.

## Scope  
Applies to permanent and fixed-term employees in FinSight’s Australian operations. Casual employees may access mandatory training at manager discretion.

## Policy Details  

### 1. Training Types  
- **Mandatory Training**: Compliance (WHS, cybersecurity, anti-discrimination), required for all employees, delivered via FinSight’s Epsilon Software Suite.  
- **Role-Specific Training**: Enhances job performance (e.g., Azure certifications for IT, analytics for Finance).  
- **Professional Development**: Employee-initiated (e.g., conferences), subject to budget approval.  

### 2. Eligibility  
- Permanent/fixed-term employees with 3+ months’ service eligible for funded training.  
- Casual employees limited to mandatory training.  

### 3. Approval Process  
- **Request**: Submit via Epsilon Software Suite HR portal with course details and business case.  
- **Criteria**: Alignment with FinSight’s goals (e.g., R&D innovation, Sales growth).  
- **Timeline**: 4 weeks prior for external training; internal schedules published quarterly.  
- **Outcome**: Up to $2,000 per employee annually, pro-rated for part-time.  

### 4. Completion Requirements  
- **Attendance**: Full participation required; non-attendance may require cost repayment.  
- **Feedback**: Submit post-training report within 2 weeks via HR portal.  
- **Certification**: Provide proof for external courses.  

### 5. Study Leave  
- **Entitlement**: Up to 5 days unpaid study leave per year for approved qualifications, per Fair Work Act 2009.  
- **Application**: Submit via HR portal with enrollment evidence.  

## Responsibilities  
- **Employees**: Identify training aligned with FinSight’s mission, apply learnings.  
- **Managers**: Support team needs, align with department goals (e.g., Operations, Marketing).  
- **HR**: Coordinate via Azure-hosted systems, track completions.  

## Compliance  
Aligns with ASQA standards. Non-compliance (e.g., misuse of funds) may result in disciplinary action.  

## Contact  
Contact HR at hr@finsight.com.au or via Epsilon Software Suite.
