# FinSight Employee Contract Policy

*Effective Date: 1 January 2025*  
*Last Updated: 31 May 2025*  
*Document ID: HR-POL-003*  

## Purpose  
To outline employment contract terms for FinSight’s 1,000 Australian employees, ensuring compliance with the Fair Work Act 2009 and alignment with FinSight’s global, cloud-native operations.

## Scope  
Applies to all permanent, fixed-term, and casual employees in FinSight’s Australian offices.

## Policy Details  

### 1. Contract Types  
- **Permanent**: Full-time (38 hours/week) or part-time, with NES entitlements, supporting Sales, R&D, and other teams.  
- **Fixed-Term**: Project-based (e.g., FinSight Cloud 2.0 development), with similar entitlements.  
- **Casual**: Variable hours, 25% loading, common in Operations.  

### 2. Contract Components  
- **Position and Duties**: Aligned with department (e.g., Finance, IT), adaptable to business needs.  
- **Remuneration**: Base salary/hourly rate, 11.5% superannuation, discretionary bonuses (tracked via CoA 40100/40200).  
- **Hours**: Full-time (38 hours), part-time (agreed), or casual (variable). Overtime per awards.  
- **Probation**: 6 months for permanent/fixed-term, with reviews at 3 and 6 months.  
- **Termination**:  
  - Permanent/Fixed-Term: 1–4 weeks’ notice (NES).  
  - Casual: No notice unless specified.  
  - Misconduct may lead to summary dismissal.  

### 3. Contract Issuance and Signing  
- **Offer**: Issued via Epsilon Software Suite HR portal.  
- **Acceptance**: Signed within 7 days, stored in Azure-hosted PostgreSQL.  
- **Digital Copies**: Employees receive PDF copies.  

### 4. Variations  
- **Changes**: Role/hours varied with mutual agreement, 4 weeks’ notice.  
- **Enterprise Agreements**: Align with FinSight’s agreements where applicable.  

### 5. Confidentiality and Non-Compete  
- **Confidentiality**: Protect FinSight’s data (e.g., Alpha Widget designs, Epsilon Suite code).  
- **Non-Compete**: Applies to senior roles (e.g., R&D), up to 6 months post-employment.  

## Responsibilities  
- **Employees**: Adhere to contract terms, sign promptly.  
- **HR**: Ensure compliance, store contracts in Azure systems.  
- **Managers**: Clarify role expectations during onboarding.  

## Compliance  
Aligns with Fair Work Act 2009, NES, and awards. Breaches may result in termination.
