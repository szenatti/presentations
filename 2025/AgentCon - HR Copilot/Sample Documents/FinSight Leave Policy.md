# FinSight Leave Policy

*Effective Date: 1 January 2025*  
*Last Updated: 31 May 2025*  
*Document ID: HR-POL-001*  

## Purpose  
This policy outlines leave entitlements for FinSight’s 1,000 employees across Australian offices, ensuring compliance with the Fair Work Act 2009 and National Employment Standards (NES). It supports FinSight’s mission to empower employees with accessible HR tools, such as the HR Leave Assistant, for managing leave balances and policies.

## Scope  
Applies to all permanent, fixed-term, and casual employees in FinSight’s Australian operations, unless specified in enterprise agreements or contracts.

## Policy Details  

### 1. Annual Leave  
- **Entitlement**: Full-time employees receive 20 days (4 weeks) of paid annual leave per year, pro-rated for part-time employees. Casual employees receive a 25% loading in lieu of paid leave.  
- **Accrual**: Accumulates progressively based on hours worked, carrying over if unused.  
- **Request Process**:  
  - Submit via FinSight’s HR Leave Assistant or Epsilon Software Suite HR portal, 14 days in advance.  
  - Approval considers operational needs (e.g., Sales and R&D project cycles).  
  - Minimum increment: 0.5 days.  
- **Payout**: Cash-out allowed with written agreement, retaining at least 4 weeks, per NES.  
- **Shutdown Periods**: FinSight may direct leave during closures (e.g., Christmas), with 4 weeks’ notice, communicated via the HR portal.  

### 2. Sick Leave (Personal/Carer's Leave)  
- **Entitlement**: 10 days paid personal/carer’s leave per year for full-time employees, pro-rated for part-time. Casual employees receive unpaid carer’s leave.  
- **Purpose**: For personal illness/injury or caring for immediate family/household members.  
- **Evidence**: Medical certificate or statutory declaration required for absences >2 days.  
- **Accrual**: Carries over if unused.  
- **Notification**: Notify manager or HR via the HR Leave Assistant before shift start.  

### 3. Parental Leave  
- **Entitlement**: Employees with 12 months’ service receive up to 12 months unpaid parental leave (birth/adoption), per NES.  
- **Paid Leave**: FinSight provides 8 weeks paid leave (primary carer) or 2 weeks (secondary carer) at base rate for permanent employees.  
- **Request**: Submit via HR portal 10 weeks prior, with 4 weeks’ confirmation.  
- **Return to Work**: Guaranteed return to pre-leave or comparable role (e.g., Sales, R&D).  

### 4. Long Service Leave  
- **Entitlement**: Per state laws (e.g., 8.67 weeks after 10 years in NSW).  
- **Accrual**: Pro-rated for part-time; casuals may qualify with continuous service.  
- **Access**: Contact FinSight HR via hr@finsight.com.au for eligibility.  

### 5. Other Leave Types  
- **Compassionate Leave**: 2 days paid per occasion for death/life-threatening illness of family/household member.  
- **Community Service Leave**: Unpaid for jury duty/emergency services; paid jury duty up to 10 days (NES).  
- **Domestic Violence Leave**: 5 days unpaid per year, per NES.  

## Responsibilities  
- **Employees**: Use HR Leave Assistant for requests and provide evidence.  
- **Managers**: Approve/deny fairly, considering FinSight’s operational needs (e.g., Operations, Sales).  
- **HR**: Maintain records in Azure-hosted PostgreSQL, ensure NES compliance.  

## Compliance  
Aligns with Fair Work Act 2009. Breaches (e.g., falsified requests) may lead to disciplinary action, managed by FinSight’s HR team.  

## Contact  
Contact HR via hr@finsight.com.au or the HR Leave Assistant.
