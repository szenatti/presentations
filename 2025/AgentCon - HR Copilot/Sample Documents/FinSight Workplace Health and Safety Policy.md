# FinSight Workplace Health and Safety Policy

*Effective Date: 1 January 2025*  
*Last Updated: 31 May 2025*  
*Document ID: HR-POL-004*  

## Purpose  
To ensure a safe workplace for FinSight’s Australian employees, contractors, and visitors, supporting the company’s cloud-native and distributed operations, in compliance with the Work Health and Safety Act 2011 (Cth).

## Scope  
Applies to all employees, contractors, and visitors in FinSight’s Australian offices.

## Policy Details  

### 1. WHS Responsibilities  
- **Employer**: Provide safe workplaces, training, and PPE, managed via Azure-hosted systems.  
- **Employees**: Follow safety procedures, report hazards via Epsilon Software Suite.  
- **WHS Committee**: Meets quarterly, includes reps from Sales, Operations, and IT.  

### 2. Risk Management  
- **Hazard Reporting**: Report via HR portal or to WHS officer immediately.  
- **Risk Assessments**: Annual assessments, covering ergonomic risks for R&D and psychosocial risks for Sales.  
- **Emergency Procedures**: Biannual fire drills, documented in Azure systems.  

### 3. Training and Awareness  
- **Mandatory Training**: Annual WHS induction (first aid, fire safety, ergonomics) via Epsilon Software Suite.  
- **Specialized Training**: For Operations roles (e.g., Alpha Widget/Beta Gadget handling).  

### 4. Incident Reporting  
- **Process**: Report incidents within 24 hours via HR portal.  
- **Investigation**: WHS officer investigates within 48 hours; serious incidents reported to SafeWork Australia.  
- **Support**: EAP available for post-incident support.  

### 5. Mental Health and Wellbeing  
- **Support**: 24/7 EAP via hr@finsight.com.au.  
- **Workload Management**: Managers monitor stress, especially in high-pressure Sales/R&D roles.  
- **Policies**: Zero tolerance for bullying/harassment, enforced via HR processes.  

## Compliance  
Complies with Work Health and Safety Act 2011. Non-compliance may result in disciplinary action.  

## Contact  
Contact WHS officer at whs@finsight.com.au or via Epsilon Software Suite.
